Meet Will Stein

Although if you ask him, he'll tell you that he grew up privileged-knowing he was lucky enough to have both a father and a mother who put family first, who each lived exemplary lives of living for others, and taught their children. through their actions.

As a child, Will Stein was moved to a new home and enrolled in a new school every year, as his parents looked to escape debt and inch their way to providing for him a better life. He finally arrived at Prince George's County at the age of fourteen, when his family was offered a temporary bedroom in the home of a fellow church member, where they resided for a year.
